Dashie's Special Tuna Dish

Description

A tuna can hodled by fresh vegetables adorned with the most harmonious seasonings.

Ingredients:

  • tuna can
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2tbs of lemon juice
  • 1/4 chappoed red onion
  • cumin

process:

  1. Start by washing then chopping the vegetables.
  2. Next add the cumin and lemon juice.
  3. Then open the tuna can and drain it of the liquid.
  4. Lastly, empty the tuna on the seasoned vegetables.
  5. Stir, grab a spoon and dig in!
